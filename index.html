Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know VBA to use Oppster?

No. Oppster is designed as a workbook-based application. You use buttons, forms, ledgers, and dashboards without needing to write VBA.

What versions of Excel are supported?

Oppster is built for desktop Microsoft Excel with macro support. Excel for the web is not recommended because VBA macros are required.

What happens if I cancel?

Your trade data remains in your workbook. Canceling stops access to paid features at the end of your current billing period, but it does not remove your historical trade records.

Is my trading data secure?

Yes. Your trade data stays in your Excel workbook. Oppster only checks subscription status when needed and does not sync your trade history to our servers.
