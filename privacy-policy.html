Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Oppster respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Oppster collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website, use our services, purchase a subscription, or contact us for support.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Name and email address when you sign up, contact us, or request support.
  • Payment and subscription information processed through our payment provider.
  • Basic website usage information such as pages visited, browser type, and device information.
  • Information you voluntarily submit through support, feedback, or feature request forms.

2. Information Stored in Your Oppster Workbook

Oppster is designed to run inside your Excel workbook. Trading activity, account details, notes, and related workbook data are stored locally in your file unless you choose to share that information with us for support purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

  • Provide access to Oppster products and services.
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • Respond to support requests and customer inquiries.
  • Improve the website, product experience, and onboarding process.
  • Send important product, account, or service-related communications.

4. Payments

Payments are processed by third-party payment providers such as Stripe. Oppster does not store your full credit card number or payment credentials. Payment providers may collect and process payment information according to their own privacy policies.

5. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to improve site functionality, understand usage, and support basic analytics. You can adjust cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only with trusted service providers who help operate our website, process payments, manage communications, or provide support, and only as needed to perform those services.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information we collect. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information you have provided to us, subject to any legal or business recordkeeping requirements.

9. Children’s Privacy

Oppster is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

11. Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at: support@oppster.com
